Company overview

Yandex

A leading technology company in Russia, which started as a search engine and has grown into one of the largest ecosystems. Today, Yandex offers a wide range of digital services, including taxis, car sharing, food delivery, cloud services, the voice assistant Alice, and much more.

VK

One of the largest IT holdings in Russia, including the social network VKontakte, as well as Odnoklassniki, VK Music, VK Video and other digital services. The company is actively developing a media platform, fintech and cloud solutions.

Sber

Russia's largest bank transformed into a digital ecosystem. In addition to banking services, Sber offers cloud platforms, smart devices, telemedicine and educational services.

T-Bank

A new player on the Russian fintech market, focused on digital service channels. Offers modern mobile applications and minimalism in financial management.

Ozon

One of the oldest Russian online stores that has turned into a marketplace. The company develops logistics, fintech, and business services.

Google

A global leader in internet technologies, offering dozens of services, including a search engine, YouTube, Android, Google Ads and cloud solutions. Actively invests in artificial intelligence.

Microsoft

One of the oldest technology companies, known for Windows, Microsoft Office, Azure and Xbox. Actively developing AI, corporate solutions and educational platforms.

Meta Platforms

Owner of Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp. The company is focused on developing the metaverse, augmented and virtual reality technologies, and artificial intelligence.

Amazon

The largest e-commerce company developing cloud services (AWS), streaming, logistics and the Alexa voice assistant.

Apple

Known for its iPhone, iPad, Mac products and service ecosystem, it actively implements augmented reality technologies and develops subscription services.

SkillBox

Russian online platform for additional education, offering courses in IT, design, marketing and other fields. The training format is focused on practice and mentoring support.

GitLab

An international DevOps platform that allows you to manage all stages of software development. Offers powerful CI/CD and task management tools.

Notion

An information organization tool that combines notes, databases, task lists, and team boards. Used for both personal productivity and project management.

Vercel

The company behind Next.js, which provides infrastructure for quickly deploying web applications. Loved by front-end developers for its simplicity and power.
